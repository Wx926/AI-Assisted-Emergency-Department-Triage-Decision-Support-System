How It Works

Three simple steps to faster, safer emergency triage

01
Nurse Inputs Vitals
Triage nurse enters patient vitals — heart rate, oxygen level, blood pressure, and chief complaint at point of arrival.
02
AI Analyzes & Predicts
Our hybrid AI model combines rule-based safety checks with Random Forest machine learning to predict severity and risk score instantly.
03
Queue Auto-Updates
Patients are automatically sorted by severity. Critical patients rise to the top. Doctors and supervisors get real-time visibility.

Built for Malaysia's EDs

Malaysia is experiencing rapid growth in its ageing population, placing increasing pressure on Emergency Departments nationwide. MOH operational documents identify prolonged bed waiting time as a key contributor to ED overcrowding.

Manual triage depends heavily on clinical judgment, which can vary under workload pressure. TriagePulse helps nurses triage consistently and rapidly — even during peak hours.

AI does not replace the clinician. It makes the clinician faster and more consistent.
